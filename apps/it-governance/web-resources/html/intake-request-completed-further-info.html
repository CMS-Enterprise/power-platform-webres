Update your System Profile

Keeping your System Profile up-to-date allows CMS to maintain current and accurate information about its systems, as required by legislation and department policy. You can view your current System Profile in EASi using the links below and may keep your System Profile updated by:

  • completing the yearly CMS System Census
  • emailing Enterprise Architecture at EnterpriseArchitecture@cms.hhs.gov if you notice any incorrect information
